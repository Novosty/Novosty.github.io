Pilaf with lamb, chestnuts and Govurma pomegranate

Young lamb is the basis of the meat table in Azerbaijan. We cook pilaf from the meat of young fat-tailed lambs. Like any Azerbaijani pilaf, this pilaf is made with folded long-grain rice cooked separately from meat and other ingredients.

Ingredients

  • Basmati rice300 years
  • Sour cream50 g
  • Water 100 ml (for example) plus 200 ml
  • Flour 200 g
  • Saffron 1 g
  • Chicken egg1 pc.
  • Chestnuts 250 g
  • Onion 120 g
  • Lamb pulp500 g
  • Pomegranate grain150 g
  • Olive oil60 gr
  • Ghee150 gr
  • saltby taste
  • freshly ground black pepperby taste

Step-by-step cooking recipe:

Step 1

Boil rice in salted water until half cooked. Throw away in a colander.

Step 2

Knead the dough based on eggs, flour, sour cream and water and salt. Wrap the dough in a transparent film and leave to rest for 10-15 minutes.

Step 3

Prepare saffron tincture. 1 g of saffron pour 200 ml of warm water and let it brew. For pilaf, use 2 tbsp. l.

Step 4

Peel fresh chestnuts (if you have frozen ones, they are usually already peeled).

Step 5

Rinse lamb, cut into cubes. Boil in salted water until tender.

Step 6

Peel the onion, cut into half rings. Fry in a mixture of olive oil and ghee. Then add lamb and simmer together for 20-25 minutes. Salt, pepper to taste. Add chestnuts and cook for another 10-15 minutes. At the very end, add pomegranate seeds.

Step 7

Heat the cauldron, grease the bottom with melted butter. Roll out a thin circle from the dough and line the bottom of the cauldron with it. The dough will not allow the rice to burn and will become one of its favorite parts (pilaf) in the process of cooking - a fragrant fried crust.

Step 8

Lay the rice on top of the dough. Drizzle with melted butter and 2 tablespoons of saffron tincture. Close with a cotton towel and lid. Cook over low heat for 30-40 minutes.

Step 9

Put the resulting rice, meat with chestnuts and pomegranate seeds on a plate. Decorate with the resulting dough crust.
