Salad with Korean carrot, red beans and croutons

If you don’t know which salad can be made from canned beans, feel free to cook the salad for a minute. A salad recipe with canned beans can be served on an everyday table, or you can cook a light salad with beans and ham on a festive table. Salad with ham and Korean carrots will be a godsend for housewives who are limited in time. After all, on the eve of the New Year, you need to pay attention to yourself, and then you still need to cook so many dishes for the New Year's table that you don't know where to start.

So, you can beautifully serve a delicious salad for the New Year with beans, ham and Korean carrots on a platter with lettuce leaves and you don’t have to cook expensive salads for this. Just four ingredients included in the salad minute will make it quick and easy to prepare.

Preparation:

It is enough to mix all the ingredients for the salad in ready-made form and season the salad with mayonnaise. But even here you can experiment and cook a salad with ham and Korean carrots, and if there is only half-smoked sausage in the refrigerator, prepare a salad with sausage and red beans, it will be delicious.
