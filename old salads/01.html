Korean carrot, cucumber and radish salad

Preparation:

This simple but delicious Korean salad is so refreshing. It goes deliciously with any main dishes, Korean or not. Cucumber and radish give the salad a pleasant crunch, slightly sweet, sour and spicy.

Daikon, also known as white radish, is a delicious and crunchy root vegetable, shaped like a carrot, and tastes like a mild red radish. Daikon is a source of many nutrients, especially it is rich in potassium and vitamin C, and contains folic acid, phosphorus, calcium and magnesium in small amounts. This root vegetable can be boiled and fried, as well as eaten raw. Raw daikon is especially good for making salads and snacks.

Preparing a salad from available products is very quick and easy. You need to mix grated carrots with radishes, season with garlic, hot pepper and coriander, vinegar, soy sauce and oil to taste. And then you need to give time for the Korean radish salad to infuse in the refrigerator.

Thoroughly rinse the vegetables and herbs, radish and cucumber should be cut into strips or grated. Finely chop the greens. Now you need to combine all the ingredients with Korean carrots and add salt. In a separate container, mix olive oil, lemon juice and mustard, season the salad. Mix it up. If you want to get a completely harmless salad, it is better not to overdo it with mustard. Done.
