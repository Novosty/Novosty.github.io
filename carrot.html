Korean food


Korean food have long been popular in Asian cuisine, but today they are also very popular in Europe. An easy-to-make korean carrot dish worthy of a place on the table of many families, as it is not only tasty, but also healthy. Korean carrot are prepared from fresh root vegetables, which are mixed with spices, pepper, paprika, chili, coriander, etc. To achieve the desired aroma and taste, you must definitely add a lot of garlic to the dish. All this must be poured with hot oil, it is better that it be olive, as it is very useful for the body. To make the final taste not bland, you need to add a little apple or wine vinegar.

    What are the benefits of Korean food? : Reduced risk of cardiovascular disease as their diet contains less cholesterol and saturated fat. Koreans also use healthy cooking methods such as grilling, fermenting, and stewing. Korean food also reduces the risk of cancer.

    What is the national dish of Koreans? ? - The main dish of Korean cuisine is kimichi - vegetables chopped and mixed with chilli, garlic and ginger and left to ferment. Whatever you order, kimichi will most likely be served along with it.

    Korean carrot is a Korean food ? : Korean carrot (Morkovcha) were discovered as an affordable and inexpensive substitute for regions where original Korean food (Kimchi) is not available. Outside of Korea, Korean carrot are the easiest and most affordable way to try true Korean food while staying at home and having it in your diet all the time.

    Why so much garlic? ? - Fresh garlic often ends up on the table along with herbs and other vegetables that are eaten raw. Korean food credits garlic with the ability to "get rid of cold or cold energy, filling the body with warm energy"

    What vitamins are in carrot ? - Korean carrot do not undergo heat treatment, so carrot almost completely retain all the vitamins contained in it: beta-carotene, vitamins of groups B, C, E, K, PP, and minerals: calcium, magnesium, iron, phosphorus.

    Why is carrot in Korean? ? It was based on the traditional Korean dish kimchi - cabbage with spices. However, later carrot were added to the dish. Over time, carrot turned out to be more popular and completely replaced cabbage, only carrot with spices remained.

    Are Korean carrot allowed for children ? The use of Korean spicy carrot is not recommended in the diet of children, as the manufacturer honestly warns about. In any case, Korean carrot should not be given to children - there is too much salt and seasoning in it.

    How to cut korean carrot ?
    (Tip - Buy a grater or shredder on site). If there is no special grater, then you can cut the carrot into strips of 2-3 mm using a vegetable peeler and a knife. carrot should be washed, peeled and cut into thin plates (slices) using a regular vegetable peeler. Cut from thin end to thick end.

    How to eat carrot to better absorb vitamin A? - in order to improve the absorption of vitamin A, carrot should be seasoned with sauces containing milk fats (sour cream, mayonnaise), the combination of which helps it to be better absorbed.
